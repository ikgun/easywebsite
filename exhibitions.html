Frida Kahlo - Her Photos

Frida Kahlo – Her Photos offers a rare glimpse into the private world of the iconic Mexican artist. Through personal photographs, the exhibition unveils the depth and complexity of her life. Since debuting in Mexico City in 2009, it has captivated nearly a million visitors across twenty cities worldwide.

Georg Baselitz - Feet First

Feet First is the largest exhibition of Georg Baselitz’s work in Norway, showcasing over 80 pieces from the 1960s to today. His bold, upside-down figures, haunting landscapes, and reflections on aging reveal a raw, expressive style. Deeply inspired by Edvard Munch, Baselitz pays tribute through playful yet powerful references throughout the exhibition.

Read more at Munch Museum's page!

Munchmuseet

Edvard Munch Portraits

Edvard Munch, known as one of the greatest portrait artists of the 19th and 20th centuries, created powerful portraits of family, friends, and figures from all walks of life. Edvard Munch Portraits is the first UK exhibition dedicated to this often-overlooked aspect of his work, showcasing portraits painted both for commissions and personal reasons, many of which transcend their subjects to explore universal themes of the human experience.
